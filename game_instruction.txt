Learn how to play :

Aim:
The aim of the game is to defeat the enemy using skills and upgrade to the next level. 
The difficulty of the game will increase with each level.
Try your best to reach the highest level.

Rules:
At the beginning of the game, skills will be assigned randomly to players. 
Players can attack the enemy using their basic attack or use the acquired skills to attack. 
Each skill corresponds to a different ability.

When player defeat boss, they will receive a reward. 
They can either choose one type of reward:
1. Strengthen the basic value of the character
2. Attain new skills (maximum of 4 skills)

When player reach level 3,5,7, they can receive both reward.
They can also save their game record.
The reward will be given randomly to players based on their choice.

At the end of each level, players can save their game progress and re-enter the game from the main menu.
